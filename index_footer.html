UI UX stands for User Interface (UI) and User Experience (UX) which are very important in the development of digital products such as applications and websites. UI UX itself is actually two different things, but the existence of the two cannot be separated. If only one aspect is left out, the product developed will not get maximum results. UX (User Experience) is the process of designing to develop product quality based on a human-first approach. The thing to remember is, UX is not about visual design;
But related to the ease, relevance, and efficiency of using a product by users (product users). Designers will think about the wants and needs of users when using the product created.UI (User Interface) is the process of optimizing the visual design of a digital product based on a human-first approach. The focus is on the beauty of the appearance and interactivity of a digital product in the eyes of the user. So that when users interact with the product, the whole process feels easy and fun. Unlike UX which can be applied to a wide variety of products, pure UI is only applied in digital products. So, such digital products have a consistent, coherent look, and are aesthetically pleasing to the eye. Components that UI designers pay attention to include icons, buttons, typography, color schemes, images, animations, object size comparisons, and other interactive visuals.

6 IMPORTANT COMPONENTS OF THE USER EXPERIENCE


1) Usability = the extent to which a product can be used by certain users to achieve certain goals efficiently, effectively, and users become satisfied when using the product.
2) Interaction Design= the design of interactions between users and a web or mobile product.
3) Visual Design= When you have designed what kind of interaction you want in your website or mobile application
4) information Architecture= a UX design component that should not be forgotten
5) Debugging= Pelajari cara melakukan pengujian dan debugging pada situs webmu.
6) Content Strategy= Another important component that you should not miss is content strategy. This component is very important because content strategy focuses on planning, creating, delivering, and managing the content of a website or mobile application
